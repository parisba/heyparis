We spent today in some of the forests up in lutruwita/Tasmania's Central Highlands that are being logged, or are about to be. It was a [Bob Brown Foundation Threatened Forest Open Day](https://bobbrown.org.au/event/threatened-forest-open-day/). It was sobering and a bit sad. Also fascinating, and the forest is beautiful.

{{< photo src="DSCF2480.JPG" alt="People gathered under a Bob Brown Foundation marquee in the rain, holding a yellow 'Save Wentworth Hills' banner with two painted owls." caption="The marquee, set up in the forest." >}}

It rained pretty much all day. The marquee was up in the middle of the forest and we stood under it while a few people talked. They went through what's happening to these forests and what's scheduled to be cut. I got a lot out of it.

Then we headed further in.

{{< photo src="DSCF2390.JPG" alt="A group of people in raincoats walking single file through dense, mossy forest, stepping over fallen logs." caption="Walking in." >}}

The trees are huge. Moss and lichen run up the trunks and you have to crane your neck to see the top.

{{< photo src="DSCF2407.JPG" alt="Looking straight up into the tall canopy of a wet eucalypt forest." caption="The canopy." >}}

It's thick down at ground level too, with stumps that have moss growing over them.

{{< photo src="DSCF2332.JPG" alt="The forest floor and understorey, with slender trunks, moss, and a small weathered stump among the greenery." caption="The understorey." >}}

A lot of this is on the list to be logged. You don't have to imagine it, they've already done it next door.

{{< photo src="DSCF2352.JPG" alt="A wide panorama of a clearfelled hillside: bare red soil, scattered slash and fallen timber, with intact forest still standing on the far ridge." caption="A clearfelled coupe, with intact forest still standing beyond." >}}

Some of these trees are hundreds of years old. The cleared block beside them is where they end up.

There was live music out in the forest too, which I wasn't expecting and really liked.

Go to one of these if you get the chance.
